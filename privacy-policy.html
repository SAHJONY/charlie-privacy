Privacy Policy for Charlie

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Important: This app is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Charlie does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. The app processes photos and videos locally on your device or via secure, encrypted API calls to third-party AI services.

2. How We Use Your Data

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data usage to report. All video generation happens either:

  • On-device: Processing occurs directly on your Android device.
  • Secure APIs: Encrypted requests to AI video generation services (no personal data is stored).

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to third parties. Your photos and videos remain yours alone.

4. Third-Party Services

Charlie may use third-party AI services for video generation. These services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend reviewing. We ensure all connections are encrypted (HTTPS).

5. Children's Privacy

Charlie is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

Email: support@charlie.videoai

This privacy policy is effective as of May 3, 2026.
